About Us

Quick Truck Service LLC is one of the leading companies delivering convenient roadside truck repair services to our esteemed consumers. Situated in Sacramento, California, next to the 99 Highway (Mack Road), we offer the best and most cost-effective truck services as defined by our customers. Our full-service shop provides solutions for all truck and trailer-related issues. We have professional and skilled mechanics who are there to help people get through their truck issues in a timely fashion. We have been serving this industry for many years and ensure our customers get back on the road fast and safely.

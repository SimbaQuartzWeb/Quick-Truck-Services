Privacy Policy

At Quick Truck Service, we make efforts to maintain the privacy of our customers. This privacy policy applies to our online activities and is valid for the users who visit our website and the information they share or collect from our website. This policy is not appropriate for any information accumulated offline or via other media than this website.

If you have any other queries or need more knowledge about our Privacy Policy, feel free to get in touch with us.

Consent

When you use our website, you hereby agree to our Privacy Policy and approve its terms.

Information we gather

The personal information that you need to provide and the reason will be made clear at the moment when we ask for your personal information.

In case you get in touch with us directly, we may have a few other pieces of information about you, such as your name, phone number, email address, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information that you provide to us.

When you register for an account, you will have to provide your contact information, such as your name, address, company name, email address, and telephone number.

How we use your details

We use the data we pile up in different ways, including:

  • Provide, manage, and uphold our website.
  • Enhance, develop, and personalize our website.
  • Learn and explore how our users utilize our website.
  • Create new products, elements, functionality, services,
  • Communicate with you, either directly or via one of our associates, including for customer service, to deliver you updates and other information about the website, and for marketing and promotional reasons.
  • Discover and avoid fraud.
  • Send you emails.

Log files

Quick Truck Service has a standard approach for dealing with log files. When a user visits our website, their information is logged in these files. The information accumulated by log files contains browser type, time stamp, referring and exit pages, Internet Service Provider (ISP), Internet protocol (IP) addresses, date, and perhaps the number of clicks. These are not related to any personally identifiable details. The main purpose of the information is to assess trends, collect demographic information, track users' movements, and help the site.

Cookies and web beacons

Like other websites, Quick Truck Service also uses cookies. We use cookies to store data like the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited and visitors' choices. The information is utilized to evaluate the users' experience by personalizing our web page content according to visitors' browser type and/or other information.

Advertisement Associates privacy policies

This privacy policy applies to the advertising partners of Quick Truck Service.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks utilize technologies like cookies, web beacons, or JavaScript that are utilized in their respective advertisements and links that arise on Quick Truck Service, which are sent directly to users' browsers. They will receive your IP address, and the technologies are operated to estimate the significance of their advertising campaigns and/or to customize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.

Note that Quick Truck Service has no access to or influence over these cookies that third-party advertisers use.

Third-party privacy policies

Quick Truck Service's privacy policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we recommend you consult the individual privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more precise data. It may include their rules and instructions about how to opt out of certain possibilities.

You have the option to disable cookies via your individual browser options. If you want to gather detailed information regarding cookie management with specific web browsers, you can find it on the browsers' respective websites.

GDPR data protection rights

We want to ensure that our customers are well aware of their data protection rights. Every user has permission to do the following:

  1. The right to access You have the right to demand duplicates of your personal data. We will ask for a small fee for this service.
  2. The right to erasure You have the right to ask that we remove your personal data under certain circumstances.
  3. The right to restrict processing: You can request that we limit the processing of your personal data under specific circumstances.
  4. The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we update any details you think are incorrect. You also have the right to ask us to complete the data you think is incomplete.
  5. The right to object to processing: You have the right to oppose our processing of your personal data under specific circumstances.
  6. The right to data portability: you can ask us to transmit the data that we have piled to another institution or straight to you under certain circumstances.
  7. In case you want to make a request, we have one month to answer you. If you would like to indulge in any of these rights, please reach out to us.

Children's Information

Another essential part of our priority is to add safety while children are using the internet. We motivate parents and guardians to participate in, watch, monitor, and instruct their online activity.

Quick Truck Service does not intentionally gather any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child delivered this kind of information on our website, we request that you reach out to us immediately, and we will make our best efforts to remove such information from our documents.
